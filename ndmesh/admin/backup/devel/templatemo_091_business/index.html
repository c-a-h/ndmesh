Description

This site is a web interface for the mesh network on campus, and is part of the NDmesh project.

Click the points on the map for more information on a specific router, and a thumbnail if available.

Use the Thumbnails link to view clickable thumbnails that will redirect you to a page with more controls over that particular camera.

The Video link will let you pick a camera to view live streams from.

The REU Wiki link takes you to the wiki for the research project.

Other links are for testing purposes, and may or may not work.
